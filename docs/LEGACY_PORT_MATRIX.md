## Enemies / AI / Perception / Stealth

*ported:13, partial:6, missing:5, spec-only:1*

The NPC AI system is substantially ported to Godot with core mechanics working: 8 NPC types (ped, thug, gunner, cop, swat, hunter, elder, thrall, rat) with 6 elite affixes are fully implemented with stat scaling. AI states (wander/chase/flee/investigate/search/guard/follow/attack/fed/downed/carried) and perception mechanics (can_see_player with LOS, exposure scaling, faction-aware threat detection) are deterministic and tested. However, the optional stealth system is partially absent: takedown mechanics (findStealthTarget, knockOut), dumpster/manhole disposal (disposeBody, nearestDumpSpot), and isLit shadow mechanics lack Godot implementations. Body carrying system exists but lacks stealth-specific body discovery avoidance (shadow stashing). Last-known-position tracking and investigation zones work; responder spawning scales with Heat stars. No NPC-vs-NPC combat melee exists in legacy or Godot. Morale mechanics (gang members fleeing when outnumbered), flank AI convergence, berserk/retaliation states, and sniper holdback are missing in Godot despite legacy precedent.
